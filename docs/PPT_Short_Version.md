# Slide 2: Introduction and Problem

## Introduction

- E-learning provides education through the internet.
- Students need a simple platform for courses and videos.

## Problem

- Learning content is often scattered.
- Students do not get one centralized learning platform.
- Existing systems may be costly or complex.

---

# Slide 3: Objectives

## Objectives

- To build a web-based E-Learning Platform
- To provide registration and login
- To show free and paid courses
- To support embedded free videos
- To provide student and admin access
